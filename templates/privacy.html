DBL Free Privacy Policy


DBL Free is privacy focused, and takes care to collect as little necessary information as possible. DBL Free will never collect personal information without your consent and intent to share. DBL Free does collect names and emails purely for the purpose of communication, and is 100% optional.

The only personal information DBL Free will ever ask for is your name and email when using the Contact Form at https://dblfree.com{% url 'contact' %}, or the Subscription Form at https://dblfree.com{% url 'subscribe' %}.

DBL Free will never share any of your information with any third party or other users that you provide in either of these two forms, and never leaves the DBL Free database, unless it is being used specifically by DBL Free to communicate with you. All of your personal information can be removed completely upon request.

DBL Free uses cookies. Signed cookie-based sessions are used specifically and exclusively for authorization to view restricted content. DBL Free also uses cookies for protection against Cross-Site Request Forgery attacks. No other cookies are used by DBL Free for any other reason.

DBL Free does employ the use of two third party plugins (namely hCaptcha and Hyvor Talk), and extensive care and research was taken to ensure that they were the best options available to protect your privacy. You can see their privacy policies at:

hCaptcha Privacy Policy: https://www.hcaptcha.com/privacy

Hyvor Talk Privacy Policy: https://hyvor.com/privacy-policy
